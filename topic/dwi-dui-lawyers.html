Houston DWI and DUI Lawyers

Arrested for Houston DUI (Driving Under the Influence) or Houston DWI (Driving While Intoxicated)? You must take this matter seriously as penalties may involve losing your license, fines, or even jail time. Houston DWI/DUI lawyers can help protect your rights and fight for the best possible outcome in your case.

Understanding DWI and DUI in Texas

In Texas, DWI (Driving While Intoxicated) is the primary charge for adults operating a vehicle with a blood alcohol concentration (BAC) of 0.08% or higher. DUI (Driving Under the Influence) typically applies to minors under 21 with any detectable amount of alcohol in their system. Both charges carry serious consequences that can affect your driving privileges, employment, and future opportunities.

The penalties for DWI in Texas increase with each offense. A first-time DWI is typically a Class B misdemeanor, but subsequent offenses or cases involving injury to others can result in felony charges. Additional factors such as having a child passenger or extremely high BAC levels can also enhance the severity of charges.

How Houston DWI Lawyers Can Help

Houston DWI attorneys understand the complexities of Texas DWI law and can challenge evidence, negotiate with prosecutors, and represent you in court. They can examine whether proper procedures were followed during your stop, arrest, and testing. This includes reviewing the accuracy of breathalyzer or blood tests and questioning whether field sobriety tests were properly administered.

An experienced DWI lawyer may also help you obtain an occupational license so you can continue driving to work, school, or other essential activities while your case is pending. They can also advise you on DWI education programs and other steps that may help reduce penalties or charges.
