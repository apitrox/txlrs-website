Trusted Houston Real Estate Lawyers and Attorneys

Houston Real Estate lawyers and attorneys handle legal work related to land or construction ownership, development, litigation, tenant rights, or landlord disputes. Houston real estate attorneys may work on residential or commercial transactions. Furthermore, they also review contracts or work in planning.

Houston Real Estate Attorneys Explain Texas Real Estate Law

Real estate law governs the rights and interests of commercial and residential real estate. It also gives protections for buyers, sellers, landowners, contractors, developers, and real estate agents.

Real estate legal issues in Texas may include sales, purchases, and leasing. Furthermore, they can also handle transfers of real estate and real property. Furthermore, they include the title to real property and settlement of claims against property rights. Moreover, they can consist of landlord-tenant issues and property development. Another aspect of real estate law is zoning and land use.

What Do Houston Real Estate Lawyers Do?

A real estate lawyer is primarily involved in transactional work, most often drafting, negotiating and closing transactions that facilitate the business of real estate. Also, working with REITS (Real Estate Investment Trusts), companies that own or finance income-producing real estate. Moreover, real estate lawyers also spend a lot of time consulting with their clients on real estate issues.

Due diligence investigations can include the review of the physical details of the property. Furthermore, they can consist of an environmental inquiry (to ensure there is no contamination). Moreover, review the leases of any tenants at the property, and review of the title to make sure the seller owns the property.
