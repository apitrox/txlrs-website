Houston Medical Malpractice Attorneys and Lawyers

Houston medical malpractice attorneys assist patients that may have been harmed by the negligence of a medical care provider. A person who wants to file a claim must do so within two years of the incident. This is called the statute of limitations and applies to all medical professionals and facilities in Texas. Medical care providers in this group include: hospitals, rehabilitation facilities, nursing homes, dentists, and physicians. In addition, chiropractors, pharmacists, emergency room staff, health maintenance organizations (HMOs), and more.

Types of Texas Medical Malpractice Cases

Houston medical malpractice lawyers handle a variety of medical malpractice cases. The most common are surgical errors, anesthesia mistakes, and failure to diagnose. Surgical errors can include leaving foreign objects in the body such as surgical instruments and gauze. Other surgical errors are operating on the wrong part of the body or the wrong patient. Anesthesia errors include using too much or too little anesthesia or not monitoring a patient's condition.

Other common areas of medical malpractice claims include birth injuries, delayed diagnosis, and failure to treat. In addition, prescription drug errors, defective medical devices, and hospital acquired infections. Failure to obtain a patient's informed consent before performing a procedure is also grounds for a claim.

Consult Houston Medical Malpractice Lawyers

If you believe you have been injured due to medical negligence, consult Houston medical malpractice attorneys. An experienced lawyer can help determine if you have a valid claim and guide you through the legal process. Texas law requires that medical malpractice claims meet specific requirements before filing in court.
