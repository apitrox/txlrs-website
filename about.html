The Texas Lawyer Referral Service, or TxLRS, is a FREE service for people needing a lawyer. We have a network of hundreds of the best lawyers in Texas. Whether you have a complex legal matter or a simple problem, we can help. Contact us now!

If you are an individual needing Free Consultation Lawyers in Houston, here is a summary of how the service works for you:

  1. You contact the TxLRS by phone or by submitting an inquiry using this form →
  2. Our Services identifies qualified lawyers with expertise matching your needs.
  3. Texas Lawyer Referral Services sends you the names and contact information of qualified lawyers.
  4. You consult with one or more of the lawyers, for free.
  5. If you want to hire a lawyer, you and the lawyer discuss any terms and fees that may apply thereafter. Through the Modest Means Program, reduced fees may be available for those who qualify.

TxLRS is currently available to individuals with a case or legal matter in the Houston Texas area including Harris, Fort Bend, Montgomery, Galveston, and Brazoria counties.
We are a public service program operated for the purpose of helping to ensure that Texans have access to a qualified lawyer when needed.

TxLRS helps to assist Texans with legal matters, including family law and matters impacting family dynamics. There are two main types of legal issues: criminal matters and civil matters. In criminal cases, the government prosecutes the case. In civil cases, the person bringing the suit hires an attorney to prosecute. The best way to research your legal issue is to start looking here on our website by identifying the broad category that it falls under.

We work with lawyers, the State Bar of Texas, and several legal aid and/or legal assistance organizations in an attempt to bring legal help to Texans in need.
